Our Goal

At FreshBox we understand that it's not easy to fit a healthy diet into a hectic lifestyle. We have designed each box to give you a variety of ingredients, made to measure, for each meal.
We pride outselves in keeping fresh new ideas of recipes and ingredients, hoping to make your life easier and show off your fancy culinary skills to your friends and family.
All you need to do it select which box best suits your needs, enter your details here and you'll recieve your first FreshBox for Free!

Our Planet

We are conscious of the impact we all have on our planet, so we only use the sustainable, ethical farmers and food producers in the country. We are always looking for new, more efficient ways to transport our goods and lower our CO2 emissions all the way from the farm fields and ending at your door.
Our boxes are all made from 100% recycled cardboard boxes, as well as the the brown paper bags inside!
All food portions are made to measure so there will be minimal food and package wastage.
