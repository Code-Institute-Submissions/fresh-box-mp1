Meet Our Boxes!

What's in the box?!

Each box we've designed has its own unique personality.
Our chefs have spent a lot of time, tasting and testing all sorts of ingredients, spices and combinations to put together each one.
As you can see, each one has it's own type of contents and we've written a description below to help you choose which one is right for you!

The Variety Box

Where to start?! There isn't much out there that won't be in this box at some point. You will be receiving everything from aspargus, sweet potato, wild boar, jerk chicken, quinoa and so much more.

The Vege Box

For all of those earth loving green fingers out there. You'll get vegetables, nuts, beans and spices to bring it all to life!

The Protein Box

The perfect spotting partner. Looking to beef up or get lean, this box packed full of the muscle repair you need to get those gains. Lentils, chicken, turkey, eggs and nuts, what more do you need?

The Carbs Box

Carbs are getting a bad name, so we've made this box to get them back in your good books and will help you find the balance
It'll have rice, pasta, spinach, avacado, chicken, tomatoes.

And just so you are aware, we email you with a full list of what to expect before sending it out. If you have anything in there you definitely don't want to have then let our customer service team know in time and we'll get another one prepared!
